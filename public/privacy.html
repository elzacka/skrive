Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Summary

Skrive does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything you write stays encrypted on your device.

What we store

  • Notes and folders: Stored locally in your browser, encrypted with AEGIS-256.
  • Settings: Language and display preferences are stored locally.

What we do not do

  • We do not send any data to servers or third parties.
  • We do not use cookies.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools.
  • We do not have user accounts or login.

Encryption

All notes are encrypted with AEGIS-256 (or XChaCha20-Poly1305 as fallback) before your browser stores them. The encryption key is generated and stored locally on your device.

Your rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can:

  • Export all data using the Export function
  • Delete all data by clearing your browser's storage
  • Use the app without an internet connection
